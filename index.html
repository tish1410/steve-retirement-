Steve's Career Timeline

The Early Days: Finding the Calling

Apprenticeship & First Jobs

Following in his tailor father's footsteps, but with a twist, Steve began his journey as an apprentice upholsterer. He landed his first job at Beales of Poole, where in true Steve style, he sold not just furniture but electricals too, much to the surprise of his colleagues! He then moved to London, excelled at TANDY, and famously doubled the volume at the Croydon branch by introducing the radical "concept of selling to customers."

The DFS Era Begins

Headhunted & The Sidcup Start

Success didn't go unnoticed. Steve was headhunted by DFS and started his journey in Sidcup, immediately doubling his income. It was here he learned from the competitive "sharks" like Ali Mehmet, adopting winning strategies like numbering his order pad to hit daily targets. He also witnessed the "wild west" of retail, a far cry from the supportive atmosphere we have today!

The OKR Chapter

Record-Breaking & The Lagos Call

In 2011, Steve moved to the new OKR store and made history, finishing as "top book" on an incredible £204k opening day. This was also the scene of one of his most memorable deals: a £6.5k order taken over the phone from a customer in Lagos, Nigeria, with a cash deposit delivered by a shipper. A testament to his adaptability and sales prowess.

The Legacy: Awards & Well-Deserved Success

Sidcup Return & Final Flourish

Returning to Sidcup 5 years ago, Steve found a wonderful, supportive team. His hard work paid off personally, with a new roof thanks to company shares and a mortgage-free life in 2015. He was recognized with trips to the Millionaires Club in Paris, a heartwarming CIN award win that bought his daughter's wedding dress, and a final, fitting RETAIL VALUES AWARD in his last month. What a finish!
